Overview

Completed with Audi's intuitive design, Audi's amazing performance, and Audi's constant innovation. The Audi e-tron is a sporty and safe companion suitable for daily driving in the new era.

The SUV aspect is evident in all designs.

The most eye-catching element of the attractive Audi e-tron's front appearance is the octagonal single-frame front grille with vertical struts. The platinum gray color and wide body are design features of the Audi e-tron. The three-dimensional bonnet and matrix LED headlights complete the sporty look of the Audi e-tron.

Audi e-tron, a pure electric vehicle. Color-accented wheel arch covers and door sills clearly convey the message that the Audi e-tron is an off-road vehicle.

Useful cargo space

Whether it's a golf trip with a business partner carrying a golf bag or a weekend ski trip with the whole family carrying a suitcase, you don't have to worry about anything. Folding the rear seats doubles maximum load capacity. You can open the trunk using a foot motion, and use the loading space located under the front hood to store various tools or mobile charging cables.

Technological innovation evident from the outside

Efficiency, safety and a new type of technology and driving experience - Virtual side mirrors reduce the width of the car by about 15 cm compared to traditional exterior mirrors, and also reduce air resistance and aerodynamic noise. A small camera transmits clear images to the high-resolution OLED display located inside. In particular, you can clearly see vehicles behind you even at dusk or at night when the headlamps are turned on.

Innovative user experience

A 7-inch display with automatic brightness adjustment is located at the dashboard and door joints to increase visibility, and a proximity sensor is included to maximize usability. When you place your finger on the surface, icons are activated that allow you to move between display submenus. You can also adjust the virtual side mirrors using the toggle function.
